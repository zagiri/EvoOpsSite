Case Study: Streamlining Operations for Kiwi Fresh Produce Co-operative

Client: Kiwi Fresh Produce Co-operative
Industry: Agriculture and Horticulture

Challenge: Kiwi Fresh Produce Co-operative, a collective of local growers and producers, faced the challenge of efficiently managing their supply chain and coordinating with a diverse network of farmers across New Zealand. They required a robust system that would streamline operations, enhance transparency, and improve communication within the co-operative.

Solution:

Evo Ops stepped in to provide a tailored solution that would address Kiwi Fresh Produce Co-operative's unique requirements. We recommended the implementation of a cloud-based ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system, customized to the agricultural industry, to centralize data, improve collaboration, and automate key processes.

Implementation:

Cloud-Based ERP Implementation: Evo Ops integrated a cloud-based ERP system that allowed for real-time visibility into inventory levels, order processing, and shipment tracking. This system facilitated seamless communication between co-operative members, resulting in improved coordination and productivity.

Training and Support:

Our team provided comprehensive training to ensure all members of the co-operative were proficient in using the new system. Additionally, we established a support framework to address any queries or issues that arose during the transition period.

Results:

The implementation of the cloud-based ERP system brought about significant improvements.

Enhanced Collaboration:

The co-operative members were able to communicate and collaborate more effectively, leading to streamlined operations and increased productivity.

Real-Time Insights:

Access to real-time data enabled better decision-making, allowing the co-operative to respond promptly to market fluctuations and customer demands.

Reduced Administrative Burden:

Automation of key processes reduced the administrative workload, allowing the co-operative members to focus on their core activities.

Improved Customer Satisfaction:

With better visibility into supply chain processes, the co-operative was able to provide more accurate delivery estimates to customers, leading to improved customer satisfaction.

Client Testimonial:

"Evo Ops revolutionized the way we operate. Their expertise in agricultural technology and their commitment to understanding our unique needs made all the difference. Our co-operative is now more efficient and cohesive than ever before."

John McKenzie, Operations Manager, Kiwi Fresh Produce Co-operative.
